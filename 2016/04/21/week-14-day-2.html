What are the invariants?

  • Audio 0:50:20.104086
  • b >= 0
    • b is counting down and eventually 1 will be subtracted from 1 and the loop will fail
      • b will be equal to 0 at that point
  • a >= 0
  • t >= 0
    • starts out at 1, t can go to zero if a is 0 to begin with
  • how many steps to calculate #a^#b
    • log(b)
  • what value is being preserved?
    • trying to compute #a^#b
    • storing stuff in t and if I’m not, I’m making a bigger and bigger

    • t + a^b = #a^#b
